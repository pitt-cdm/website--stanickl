School

Right now I'm a double degree student at the University of Pittsburgh studying Theatre Arts and Media & Professional Communications.

My extra curriculars include sound designing shows for University of Pittsburgh Stages, playing alto sax in Pitt's varsity marching band, and acting in the Redeye Theatre Project festivals!

After I graduate I plan on taking a year off to work and save up a little money. I would like to apply to graduate school for acting, creative writing, or film studies.

Theatre

I love theatre. I was inspired to pursue theatre in college after I first listened to the original broadway cast soundtrack of Hamilton. It was so inspiring and made me want to pursue a career of creativity and performance.

There are so many way a person can express their creativity: acting, spoken word, dance, music. All of these types of expression can be incorporated into a theatrical performance. The space for creativity is infinite.

Right now I'm focused on sound designing and acting with University of Pittsbugh Stages. I'm honing skills and hope to be working on mainstage shows before I graduate.
